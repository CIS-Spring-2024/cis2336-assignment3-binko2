Behind Binkoria:



Bianca Panes, the passionate owner of Binkoria Cafeteria, is a culinary innovator who draws inspiration from Filipino fusion cuisine. Growing up as a 1st gen American, she was able to get the best of many worlds and flavor profiles. Wanting to share her culture with others, she strove to deliver new fusion foods to blend into the melting pot of America.

When she's not in the kitchen creating culinary masterpieces, Bianca loves to explore the world and immerse herself in different cultures. Her travels not only broaden her culinary horizons but also inspire new and exciting dishes for Binkoria Cafeteria.

A fitness enthusiast at heart, Bianca believes in the importance of a healthy lifestyle. She dedicates time to regular workout sessions, staying active and energized to pursue her culinary passion with vitality. In the realm of nutrition, Bianca specializes in crafting high-protein meals that are not only delicious but also promote health and wellness. Her dedication to creating nutritious yet flavorful dishes sets Binkoria Cafeteria apart as a destination for those seeking wholesome and satisfying dining experiences.

In her leisure time, Bianca enjoys unwinding with her favorite video games. Whether she's exploring virtual worlds or engaging in friendly competitions, gaming provides her with a fun and relaxing escape.
